Major General Aung San

The man who brought about Burma's independence

Wedding of General Aung San and His Lady Daw Khin Kyi

Here's a time line of General Aung San's life:

  • 1915 - Born on Feb 13, Natmauk, Magwe, British Burma
  • 1933 - Entered Rangoon University,quickly became student leader
  • 1938 - Left his law classes and entered national politics in October
  • 1939 - Became a founding member and the first Secretary General of the Communist Party of Burma (CPB)
  • 1940- He attended an Indian National Congress Assembly in Ramgarh, India, along with Thakins Than Tun and Ba Hein.
  • 1941 - Worked with Japanese intelligence, secretly re-entered Burma and began efforts to contact and recruit additional Burmese agents.
  • 1942 - Married to Daw Khin Gyi
  • 1947 - Assassinated by U Saw

"Every thought, every word, and every action that adds to the positive and the wholesome is a contribution to peace. Each and every one of us is capable of making such a contribution. Let us join hands to try to create a peaceful world where we can sleep in security and wake in happiness."
---General Aung San
